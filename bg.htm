BodyGuide is an app that helps people relieve pain through a holistic program that supports body literacy. The app redesign strays away from a clinical look and adopts a tone of gratitude, joy and peace.

Client—BodyGuide
Design Services—Mobile App UI, Avatars
Programs/Software—Figma
Get The App—Apple Store↗, Google Play↗

It increases engagement and strengthens the brand as it utilises the rules set out in BodyGuide's branding guidelines. As well as designing for new functions, the app considers user experiences with consistent buttons and colour coded backgrounds for each phase of the program.

Avatars were also created to go with the redesign. By using geometric shapes and playing with foreground/background, the avatars reflect a diverse user base.
